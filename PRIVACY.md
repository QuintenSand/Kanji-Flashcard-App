# Privacy Policy — 漢字フラッシュカード (Kanji Flashcard App)

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Overview

Kanji Flashcard App is a fully offline Japanese kanji study app. Your privacy is straightforward: **we collect nothing**.

## Data We Collect

**None.** The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There is no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDK, and no network requests of any kind.

## Data Stored on Your Device

All data the app generates — your SRS review history, study streak, session records, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using iOS's standard `UserDefaults` mechanism. This data never leaves your device.

You can delete all app data at any time via **Settings → Data → Reset All Progress**, or by deleting the app from your device.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses no third-party services, frameworks, or SDKs.

## Push Notifications

If you enable the optional daily reminder, the notification is scheduled locally on your device using iOS's `UNUserNotificationCenter`. No data is sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time in the app's Settings screen or in iOS Settings.

## Children's Privacy

The app contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no data collection, making it suitable for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will be posted here and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
